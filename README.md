# Chief Operating Officer - AI Adoption Candidate Vision

Independent candidate campaign by Russell Dudek for the confidential AI adoption and consulting firm represented by MDC Executive Search.

## Strategic argument

**Build the company to carry the promise.** The campaign treats the role as a founder-leverage and professional-services operating challenge: connect the sales promise to scope, blended human-and-agent capacity, delivery, documented proof, renewal, margin, cash, and a deliberate reduction in routine founder dependency.

## Routes

- `index.html` - candidate vision and interactive Engagement Load Path
- `resume.html` - exactly two-page printable resume
- `cover-letter.html` - exactly one-page printable cover letter
- `interview-brief.html` - executive interview thesis
- `120-day-plan.html` - entry plan
- `founder-operating-contract.html` - role-specific operating artifact
- `role-alignment.html` - one-page alignment brief

Every printable route provides a native PDF download. Resume and cover letter cross-link in both directions.

## Research and integrity

- `brand-intelligence.md` records the confidential-client brand decision and the official-asset retrieval exception.
- `role-intelligence.md` records the actual work, tensions, stakeholders, decisions, and first-year measures.
- `evidence-map.md` records verified transferability and claim boundaries.
- `source-notes.md` records public research provenance.
- `campaign-audit.md` records responsive, interaction, print, PDF, contact, and confidentiality validation.
- `artifact-manifest.md` records the complete publication set.

The client identity was not disclosed in the posting. This campaign does not guess publicly. Company-specific operating ideas are labeled as hypotheses for discovery. Career claims are bounded to verified candidate evidence.
