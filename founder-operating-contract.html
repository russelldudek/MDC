Draft for discovery

Standing leadership sync

Purpose: close decisions that cross market, client, operating, and capital boundaries - not replay departmental updates.

  • Market signal and strategic relationships
  • Client/portfolio exceptions above threshold
  • Forecast, margin, cash, and capacity posture
  • People / organization decisions
  • AI capability and risk decisions
  • Explicit decisions, owners, dates, and communication

Founder attention budget

Track founder involvement as a scarce strategic resource. Every recurring escalation is classified:

  • Value creating: market access, strategic relationship, differentiated judgment
  • Temporary: new mechanism still stabilizing
  • Structural defect: missing owner, decision right, information, capability, or confidence
  • Retire: routine work that should no longer route through the founder

How the COO's performance should be evaluated

Clientsretention, outcome evidence, escalation quality
Economicsmargin integrity, forecast credibility, cash discipline
Deliveryschedule, scope, quality, adoption, reuse of learning
Teammanager strength, bench readiness, onboarding speed, async discipline
AI capacityreliability, owner confidence, cost, human override, quarterly improvement
Founder leveragefewer routine dependencies without loss of market signal or decision quality
Calibrated question: What would make it safe for the founder to miss the weekly operating review for a month - and still trust the company is carrying the promise?
